
# Frontline Defense: WW2

A tactical, grid-based 3D Tower Defense game built with **React**, **Three.js**, and **React Three Fiber**.

## ğŸ® Game Overview

Defend your sector against waves of armored divisions. Strategically place fortifications, turrets, and heavy artillery to stop the enemy advance.

### Features
*   **3D Tactical Map:** diverse enemies including Infantry, Motorcycles, Light Tanks, and Heavy Panzers (Titans).
*   **Arsenal:** 6 unique tower types including MG42 Nests, Anti-Material Snipers, Rocket Artillery, and experimental Tesla coils.
*   **Physics:** Projectile ballistics and homing missiles with splash damage.
*   **Progression:** Upgrade system for tower Damage, Range, and Fire Rate.
*   **Audio:** Dynamic sound effects and spatial audio.
